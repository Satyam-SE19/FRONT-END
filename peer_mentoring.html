About Dr. Satyam

Dr. Satyam Singh is a Professor of Computer Science with over 15 years of teaching experience at the university level. He specializes in algorithms, data structures, and introductory machine learning concepts.

As a mentor, Dr. Chen focuses on helping students develop strong problem-solving skills and computational thinking. He believes in a hands-on approach to learning and provides personalized guidance based on each student's learning style.
